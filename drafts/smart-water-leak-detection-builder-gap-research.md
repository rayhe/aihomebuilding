# Research: Smart Water Leak Detection — The Builder Gap

## Core Thesis
Water damage is the #2 cause of home insurance claims (22.6%), more frequent than fire (21.9%). Average claim: $15,400. The technology to detect leaks exists, costs $200-$400, and can be installed for ~$50 in labor during construction when plumbing is exposed. Yet no building code requires it, and almost no builders install it. We mandate $30 smoke detectors for fire. We don't mandate $350 leak detectors for water — which causes more claims.

## Data Points

### Scale of the Problem
- Water damage = 22.6% of all home insurance claims 2019-2023 (III/ISO), 2nd only to wind/hail
- Average water damage claim: $15,400 (Triple-I 2019-2023 five-year average), up 10% from $13,954 (2018-2022)
- $13 billion/year in insurer payouts (NAIC)
- 14,000 water damage incidents per day in US (iPropertyManagement)
- 1 in 60 insured homes files a water damage claim per year (III/ISO)
- 1 in 15 homes faces non-weather water damage within 5-year policy period (CoreLogic)
- 40% of homeowners will suffer some type of water damage (Gabriel Halimi, CEO Flo Technologies)
- Average leak goes undetected 75+ days (EMC Security)
- Hidden leaks waste ~10,000 gallons/year per household (EPA)
- 1 inch of water = up to $25,000 damage (FEMA)
- 98% of basements experience water damage in their lifetime (iPropertyManagement)
- Fewer than 20% of homeowners take precautionary steps (ConsumerAffairs)

### Fire vs. Water Comparison
- Fire & lightning: 21.9% of claims, avg $83,991 per claim
- Water damage & freezing: 22.6% of claims (MORE FREQUENT), avg $15,400 per claim
- Smoke detectors: mandated by building code since ~1970s-80s, cost $20-50 each
- CO detectors: mandated in most states, cost $20-50 each
- Gas detectors: Connecticut mandated Jan 2023 (HB 6848)
- Water leak detectors: NOT mandated anywhere for single-family residential new construction

### The Technology
- Moen Flo Smart Water Shutoff: installs on main water line, detects leaks as small as 1 drop/minute with Microleak™ Technology, auto-shutoff capability, monitors pressure/flow/temperature
- Flo by Moen "Local Mode": works WITHOUT WiFi during construction — only needs pressurized pipes and AC power. "Many job sites don't have Wi-Fi up and running during the construction or renovation process" — Suzy Street, Moen
- Moen "Spacer Kits": allow plumbers to pre-plumb for future Flo installation during construction
- Phyn Plus: $300-400, professionally installed on main water line, AI-powered anomaly detection
- Flume: clamps on water meter non-invasively
- Bluebot: $200-600 range, no subscription

### Original Calculation — ROI During Construction
- Device cost: $250-400 (Moen Flo, Phyn Plus)
- Labor during construction (plumbing exposed): ~$50 (5-10 min plumber time)
- Total install cost during construction: $300-$450
- Retrofit install cost: $400-$900 (add $150-500 professional install)
- Annual expected loss: 1/60 probability × $15,400 = $257/year
- Annual insurance savings: 7.5% (mid-range) of $2,200 avg premium = $165/year
- Combined annual benefit: ~$422/year
- Payback period during construction: $375 / $422 = 10.7 months
- 30-year present value (3% discount): ~$8,600 in expected savings against a $375 investment
- Builder scale (D.R. Horton, 80K homes/yr): $30M/year investment protecting against ~$2B potential claims
- Compare: smoke detector (whole house): ~$200-350 installed, mandated
- Compare: CO detector (whole house): ~$100-200 installed, mandated in most states
- Water leak detector: $300-450 installed during construction, mandated NOWHERE
