# Research: AI Construction Dispute Resolution & Documentation Gap

## Journalist: Frank "The Foreman" DeLuca (Project Management & Operations)

## Angle
Construction disputes skyrocketed post-COVID (42% increase in average value, North America). Commercial builders now use AI to review 100,000+ documents at 85% lower cost. But the typical residential dispute — $15K-$50K over bad tile, missed code requirements, or a change order fight — can't afford $27,000 in document review. The real lesson for homeowners and small builders: AI documentation DURING construction prevents disputes from happening.

## Kill Test
Does this help someone building or buying a home? **YES** — homeowners and GCs need to know:
1. How common disputes are and what they cost
2. What AI tools exist for dispute documentation vs. resolution
3. What daily documentation practice prevents disputes from reaching litigation

## Primary Sources

### 1. BRG (Berkeley Research Group) — "Construction Disputes in 2025: The Year of Generative AI" (ThinkSet Magazine, Winter 2025)
- Average value of disputes in North America rose 42% from 2021 to 2022 (Arcadis Construction Disputes Report 2023)
- 70% of construction organizations expected disputes to increase over next 2 years (Burford Capital 2023 survey of construction lawyers)
- Traditional manual review of 100,000 project emails: 7 weeks, ~$216,000 (multiple attorneys)
- AI-enhanced review of same: 4 business days, ~$27,000 (85% cost/time savings)
- AI can spot patterns across thousands of daily reports: recurring weather delays, material shortages, equipment problems, design changes
- Leading firms deploying: specialized AI agents for daily reports, email analysis through case-specific parameters, intelligent tools deriving insights from case filings
- Source: https://www.thinkbrg.com/thinkset/construction-disputes-in-2025-the-year-of-generative-ai/

## The Residential Gap
The BIG story: AI dispute tools (BRG, nPlan, etc.) serve commercial mega-projects ($100M+). The residential dispute is typically:
- $15K-$50K in dispute value
- Change order disagreements, code violation discoveries, defective workmanship
- Neither party can afford $27K in document review, let alone $216K
- Most residential disputes are resolved informally, through complaint boards, or in small claims
- The ones that DO litigate bleed both parties dry

## The Actionable Angle (Required Gate)
**For homeowners:**
- Use AI-powered daily log apps (Buildots at GC level, but simpler: OpenSpace, CompanyCam) during construction — $29-$79/month
- Timestamped photo documentation every site visit: AI-searchable later
- AI contract review BEFORE signing ($50-$200 one-time vs thousands in disputes)
- Know that your GC's daily logs may be spotty or missing — 22% still use spreadsheets

**For builders:**
- AI documentation protects you FROM homeowner disputes, not just against them
- Daily photo/video logs are your insurance policy
- CompanyCam, Procore, Buildertrend all have AI features that auto-tag, organize, flag issues
- Cost of daily documentation: $30-80/month. Cost of one dispute: $15K-$150K
