About

Where AI Meets Construction

AI Home Building is an independent research publication tracking how artificial intelligence, robotics, and automation are transforming the $2.1 trillion global construction industry.

We cover the full stack — from generative design tools that produce thousands of floor plan variations in minutes, to autonomous bricklaying robots building walls in days, to AI project management platforms compressing 14-month build timelines down to 10.

But we also cover the human side: the 500,000 missing construction workers, the aging workforce, the policy gaps, and what happens to skilled tradespeople when machines can do their jobs faster and cheaper.
